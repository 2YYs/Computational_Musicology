Comparing Chroma vs. Timbre Self-Similarity Matrices
By comparing the two SSMs for 'Here Comes the Sun', the pop structure of the song is highly visible, appearing as a classic checkerboard pattern. The dark main diagonal line shows the track comparing perfectly to itself in real-time.

Timbre SSM: The timbre-based SSM also shows the Verse-Chorus-Verse structure, but the blocks are slightly noisier and less sharply defined than the chroma matrix.
